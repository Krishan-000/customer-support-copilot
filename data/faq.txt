How do I upgrade my subscription?
Go to Settings > Billing > Upgrade Plan.

Can I use SmartDesk Pro on mobile devices?
Yes, Android and iOS apps are supported.

How many agents can access the platform?
Each license is assigned to one agent.

Can I export tickets?
Yes, tickets can be exported as CSV or Excel files.

What browsers are supported?
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge.

How do I contact support?
Email support@smartdeskpro.com.

Q: How long are tickets retained?
Ticket history is retained indefinitely for paid plans.

Does the platform support integrations?
Yes, integrations are available for CRM, Slack, and Microsoft Teams.
